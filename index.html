About Us

Enloe Science Olympiad is an organization dedicated to fostering a passion for science among elementary, middle, and high school students in our local community, aiming to completely eliminate barriers in STEM imposed by gender, socioeconomic status, background, or identity. It has enjoyed a rich history and reputation having won numerous trophies and countless individual medals in the state and national level. Our team consists of two JV teams, and a varsity team. It is run by an elected 5-person board, and placement on the varsity and junior varsity teams is through a selective tryout process. If you have any questions or concerns, you can contact us at enloescienceolympiad@gmail.com
